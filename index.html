About the GoatHouse

The GoatHouse

It all started one dreary Wednesday evening at the GoatHouse (residence of the Goats in Provo, Utah). Creativity and boredom, the deadliest combo other than our pizza and Goat Horns, stepped in and gave them an idea - pizza.

Cooking Origins

The Goats began by purchasing a slew of bricks and in no time at all, they had constructed a makeshift oven. That oven is now running at triple capacity and is ready to serve you the freshest brick oven pizza this side of the Mississippi.

Order Today

Open every weekend, GoatHouse Brick Oven Pizza boasts the best homemade pizzas and friendliest service staff around! To get one step closer to our fresh pies, start your order here and see you at the Goat House!
